How to Play No Mercy Cards

Basic Rules

Match cards by color or number. Be the first to get rid of all your cards to win! Don't forget to press "1" when you have one card left!

Special Cards

  • Skip Card - Makes the next player miss their turn
  • Reverse - Changes the direction of play
  • Draw Two - Next player draws 2 cards and misses turn
  • Wild Card - Choose the next color to play
  • Wild Draw Four - Choose color and next player draws 4 cards

Frequently Asked Questions

How many players can join a game?
You can play with 2-4 players in each game.
Is the game free to play?
Yes, No Mercy Cards is completely free to play in your web browser!
Can I play on mobile devices?
Yes, the game is fully responsive and playable on all devices with a web browser.
How many cards are in a deck?
There are 108 cards in total, including number cards and special action cards.
What happens if I forget to call "One" with one card left?
You'll receive a penalty of two additional cards if you don't press "1" when you have one card remaining.
Are there rankings or leaderboards?
Yes, you can compete for positions on our global leaderboard and track your progress.
